About

Hi! I'm Kyle. I'm a writer and product manager interested in bioethics, AI, and literature. I've been a product manager for almost a decade. My most recent work was launching flagship AI products at Amazon One Medical. Before that, my team and I built tools and systems that helped make practice management easier (tasks, scheduling, check-in), improved safety with vaccine scanners (the first hardware at One Medical), and added lots of quality of life elements for our patients (like the 'running late' text option).

Current Work

I am a regular contributor to 3 Quarks Daily, where I tend to write a lot about how technology and the humanities tend to be better and more interesting together.

Personal projects include creating an MCP to let Claude Code read and work with Scrivener so that I can have a team of agents help enhance my writing process and to see if AI can be a legitimate partner in creative work. I'll be exploring serialized publishing and how things like GitHub might allow self-publishing to be higher quality and more collaborative.
