About

I'm a Senior Product Manager at Amazon working on Care AI, with a background spanning product management at Apple and One Medical, and philosophical exploration of technology ethics and bioethics.

My work sits at the intersection of practical product development and deeper questions about how technology shapes society. I've written about bioethics, transhumanism, and moral philosophy for publications including Discover Magazine and Slate.

Current Work

At Amazon, I focus on Care AI products that improve healthcare experiences. Previously, I worked on workflow and scheduling systems at One Medical and contributed to various projects at Apple.

Writing & Research

I explore the ethical dimensions of emerging technologies through my blog Pop Bioethics and as an Affiliate Scholar with the Institute for Ethics and Emerging Technologies. Recent work includes examining how technology can address moral dilemmas and the cultural impact of AI-driven narratives.
